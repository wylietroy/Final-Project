User Story
1. Attempt to get the frog to the other side
2. Avoid the moving cars
3. Reach the opposite side
4. Score 1 point and go again or fail and try and get to the other side again
5. See how high of a score you can get!

Game Description

This game is called Frogger, the goal of the game is to reach the other side as many times as possible before failing to cross.
Your goal is to cross each of the four streets without being hit by the cars that are driving by.
